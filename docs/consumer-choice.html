3.3 Consumer Choice

Consumer maximize their utility subject to the budget constraint they face. Note that the slope of the budget constraint is \[\frac{\Delta q_y}{\Delta q_x} = -\frac{p_x}{p_y}\] The marginal rate of substitution (MRS) is the slope of the indifference curve: \[MRS_{x,y} = -\frac{\Delta q_y}{\Delta q_x} \bigg \vert_{\Delta U = 0}\] So the optimality condition, i.e., optimal choice is \[MRS_{x,y} = \frac{p_x}{p_y}\]
